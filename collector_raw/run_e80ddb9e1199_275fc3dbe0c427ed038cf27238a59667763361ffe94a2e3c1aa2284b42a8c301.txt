# Three-party approval access guide

Third-party approval integration allows Feishu Approval to support the pushing of approval tasks from external systems (self-developed or third-party purchased by the enterprise) to the Feishu Approval application. Approval tasks can reach users in real-time across multiple devices (PC, mobile, Pad) and can be opened through **embedded documents in Feishu Approval**. Users can view and handle all approvals in Feishu Approval for a one-stop approval experience. Combined with features such as dialog message push, one-click forwarding in chats, daily pending reminders, and efficiency dashboards, the entire approval process from initiation to processing to statistics is comprehensively improved.

*   If you have an approval integration requirement, you can [schedule a solution demo](https://wenjuan.feishu.cn/m?t=syKbxL1vcOsi-1x4e).
*   To learn how to use Feishu Approval, refer to the [Feishu Approval Admin Manual](https://www.feishu.cn/hc/zh-CN/articles/360033971554#magicdomid-1_3114).

## Suitable scenarios

In medium to large enterprises, approvals are often scattered across different business systems. Approvers need to spend time every day switching between different systems to handle approvals, which is cumbersome and time-consuming. Moreover, approvers can easily forget tasks, causing delays in business processing and affecting management efficiency. Senior managers face more types and quantities of approvals, worsening the problem.

![Image 2](https://sf3-cn.feishucdn.com/obj/open-platform-opendoc/d6340d335ca012f861e9224ba1587545_BIEe8XT4GH.png?height=1734&lazyload=true&maxWidth=600&width=3200)

Feishu Approval offers a lightweight integration method, eliminating the need for enterprises to modify their existing business systems. Businesses can simply aggregate and push approval tasks to Feishu Approval at the data layer. Approvers can then view and handle all approval tasks in a "one-stop" manner, enhancing their processing efficiency through daily pending reminders, quick approvals, automatic list clustering, and other features.

![Image 3](https://sf3-cn.feishucdn.com/obj/open-platform-opendoc/67ed9d876089e6d2e696cba903394ce0_qXWIJz7cvv.png?height=1734&lazyload=true&maxWidth=600&width=3200)

## Value of integration

*   **The approval center aggregates scattered approval documents, reducing the inconvenience of multiple entry points, creating a "one-stop" approval management center**

    *   Integrates documents across various enterprise systems like OA, HR, and Finance into the approval center list, providing a unified and convenient operation and query method.
    *   The approval center supports opening third-party system approval pages in embedded or side drawer forms, reducing page navigation for approvers, offering a seamless approval experience.
    *   The page structure and design are simple, adapted for PC, mobile, and Pad, allowing seamless multi-device switching, with low promotion and onboarding barriers.

![Image 4](https://sf3-cn.feishucdn.com/obj/open-platform-opendoc/49b9eccbd1ff65d6bd3826fab8d021a9_VMStEyycHf.png?height=1944&lazyload=true&maxWidth=600&width=5718)

*   **Integrating into the approval center empowers business efficiency, facilitating faster approval circulation**

    *   Integrated pending approval tasks are aggregated and pushed daily via bot messages, reducing approval circulation time and minimizing prolonged approval tasks.
    *   Integrated approvals can be quickly forwarded to chats, allowing approvers to complete approvals during communication.
    *   Approval circulation details are aggregated into the efficiency dashboard, visualizing management efficiency improvements.

![Image 5](https://sf3-cn.feishucdn.com/obj/open-platform-opendoc/9269da5aa613524e91711e279c30914f_bIWH5pwn2n.png?height=1716&lazyload=true&maxWidth=600&width=2500)

*   **Provides unified, simple approval operations, offering a more relaxed approval experience**

    *   Built within the Feishu ecosystem, connections between systems are better, extending the usage scenarios.
    *   Enhances processing efficiency from the approver's perspective through automatic clustering, marking for later processing, etc., offering a more relaxed approval experience.

## Best practice

Previously, approvals at ByteDance were spread across 23 systems. Now, approvers can perform "one-stop approvals" through the approval center:

*   The time to complete all nodes of an approval has been reduced by **48%** since integrating into the approval center.
*   Aggregating and reminding of pending approvals can avoid missing documents due to dispersed approval environments, significantly improving the 24-hour message read rate by **6.62%**.
*   With approximately **25,000+** approval tasks completed daily at ByteDance, the efficiency improvement is significant.

## Integration methods and costs

### Method 1: Using Feishu approval’s integration tool

Feishu's official self-developed connector for interfacing with third-party OA systems, designed to meet the need for low-cost integration of third-party approvals within Feishu.

#### Customer costs

Standard capabilities are available for deployment without additional development, supported by professional implementation personnel.

#### Integration method

[Official Approval Connector](https://open.feishu.cn/document/ukTMukTMukTM/ukjNyYjL5YjM24SO2IjN/official-approval-connector) currently only supports some frequently used third-party OA systems, some of which require a fee. You can [click here to contact the Feishu Approval team for integration support](https://wenjuan.feishu.cn/m?t=syKbxL1vcOsi-1x4e).

### Method 2: Self-service integration through open capabilities

You or your partners can use Feishu Approval's open capabilities ([OpenAPI and Events](https://open.feishu.cn/document/uAjLw4CM/ukTMukTMukTM/reference/approval-v4/approval-overview#6a043c9e)) to complete data integration between the third-party approval system and Feishu Approval Center, enabling use of approval functions within Feishu.

#### Customer costs

The integration, conducted by you or your chosen third-party technical partner, typically takes 2-4 weeks.

#### Integration method

Call the approval API to associate data between the third-party approval system and Feishu Approval Center. The actual approval process remains in the third-party system, but the approval status changes can be synchronized to Feishu Approval in real-time. The initiator and reviewer can complete approval-related operations in a "one-stop" manner within Feishu. The integration process is shown in the following diagram:

![Image 8](https://sf3-cn.feishucdn.com/obj/open-platform-opendoc/2483ce018928f46965e6da9efe172e8b_ksrfwnbJXb.png?height=884&lazyload=true&maxWidth=600&width=953)

#### Process overview

1.   The enterprise administrator or developer first needs to call the [Create Third-Party Approval Definition](https://open.feishu.cn/document/uAjLw4CM/ukTMukTMukTM/reference/approval-v4/external_approval/create) interface to associate the third-party approval system with the Feishu Approval Center. This includes the unique identifier, group, name, and description of the approval process within Feishu, the third-party approval link to be accessed during submission in Feishu, and the callback URL of the third-party approval system (used to receive changes in approval tasks within Feishu).

2.   The approval submitter initiates an approval in the Feishu client.

During this process, the developer needs to call the [Synchronize Third-Party Approval Instances](https://open.feishu.cn/document/uAjLw4CM/ukTMukTMukTM/reference/approval-v4/external_instance/create) interface to synchronize the approval instances, tasks, and cc data within the third-party approval system.

3.   When the approval reaches a designated node, the approver handles the corresponding approval task.

During this process, the developer needs to call the [Send Approval Bot Message](https://open.feishu.cn/document/ukTMukTMukTM/ugDNyYjL4QjM24CO0IjN) interface to push approval message notifications to approvers in the Feishu client. Approvers can complete the approval directly within the notification card.

4.   The developer continues to call the [Synchronize Third-Party Approval Instances](https://open.feishu.cn/document/uAjLw4CM/ukTMukTMukTM/reference/approval-v4/external_instance/create) interface to update the status of approval instances and tasks. Finally, they call the [Verify Third-Party Approval Instances](https://open.feishu.cn/document/uAjLw4CM/ukTMukTMukTM/reference/approval-v4/external_instance/check) interface to ensure that the latest approval status is synchronized to the Feishu Approval Center.

#### Getting started

Feishu Open Platform provides scenario tutorials based on open capabilities for integrating third-party approvals. You can refer to [Quickly Develop Third-Party Approvals](https://open.feishu.cn/document/home/quickly-develop-three-party-approvals/introduction) to experience the integration process.

## FAQ

### How are Feishu Approvals and third-party systems integrated? Do we need to migrate the entire process?

No, you don't. Feishu Approvals uses a lightweight integration method, only connecting at the data level. The pages opened in Feishu Approvals still display the document details from the third-party system, reducing integration costs and providing a seamless one-stop view of approval process data.

### What kinds of approvals can be integrated with Feishu Approvals?

Whether it is a self-built enterprise approval system or a purchased third-party approval system, as long as the enterprise can access the approval task data, it can be integrated with Feishu Approvals. Self-built systems can theoretically all be integrated. Among third-party systems, our official approval connectors can already achieve quick integration with **Panweaver OA** and **Seeyon OA** systems, and other OA systems can also be integrated through the open approval capabilities.

### What are the advantages of the Feishu Approval integration solution compared to an enterprise's self-developed OA?

*   Deep integration with Feishu Suite: An enterprise's self-developed OA and Feishu Suite are usually separated, merely adding an application within Feishu. However, Feishu Approval, as an official application, can be more deeply integrated with the Feishu Suite, providing a better experience and higher efficiency. Feishu Approval supports approvals directly through Feishu messages, cloud documents, and emails, bringing the approval actions closer to where the behavior happens.
*   Continuous optimization of approval efficiency and signal-to-noise ratio: Enterprises' self-developed OAs often cannot allocate significant resources for continuous optimization after going live. In contrast, the Feishu Approval team continuously invests in improving efficiency and signal-to-noise ratio, aiming to create "one of the most efficient approval systems".
