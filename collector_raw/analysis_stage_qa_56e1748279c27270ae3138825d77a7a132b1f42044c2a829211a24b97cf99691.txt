# [](https://open.wps.cn/documents/app-integration-dev/wps365/server/introduce#product-introduction)Product Introduction

## [](https://open.wps.cn/documents/app-integration-dev/wps365/server/introduce#overview)Overview

WPS365The product serves as a new productivity platform for office environments, comprehensive for one-stopAIoffice scenarios, aiming to help enterprises or organizations improve productivity.WPS365Composed ofWPS Office、WPS AIEnterprise Edition,WPScollaboration suites, including cloud documents, AirPage, messaging and conversations, email, to-do, calendar, meetings,OFFICEclient applications, and other business domains.[Click here to learn moreWPS365](https://365.wps.cn/)

Within enterprises or organizations, besidesWPS 365there are usually otherITsystems such asOA、CRM、ERPvarious industry-specific systems within the organization. These systems, along withWPS365collectively bear the responsibility of digital operation for the organization, and therefore typically need to**integrate, coordinate**to provide employees with a better daily usage experience and further advance digital transformation for the enterprise or organization.

WPS365The application targets these scenarios, with each business domain offering extensive server-sideWPS 365 OpenAPIclient applications,JSAPIcallback events, etc., providing otherITsystems withWPS 365business within.

## [](https://open.wps.cn/documents/app-integration-dev/wps365/server/introduce#common-business-domains)common business domains

WPS365has nearly20business domains. Below introduces several typical business domains and their open capabilities.

**Contacts**

refers to the WPS365 account system, with an enterprise address book including departments, users, user groups, etc. On the address book side, WPS365 offers rich management capabilities for querying, creating, deleting, updating, and moving departments/users, enabling synchronization of the WPS365 account system with existing enterprise account systems, single sign-on scenarios, etc. It also provides change events for departments/users, allowing third-party systems to subscribe and monitor WPS365 account status changes.

**Cloud Documents**

It refers toWPS365Online document management capabilities, cloud documents can be stored in different folders under personal space and team space. Cloud documents offer file (folder) creation management, file upload and download, file sharing, file permission management, and full lifecycle management capabilities. Enterprises can integrate third-party systems with cloud documents, such as automatically saving attachments from the email system to cloud documents orOAuploading files directly from the cloud documents in the system.

**Messages and Conversations**

Based onWPSInstant Messaging Provided by WPS Teams,WPS365Opened capabilities for group chat management, sending messages, querying messages, etc., allowing enterprises to leverage these capabilities to establish more timely and efficient communication channels for various businesses. For example, in a ticketing system, you can connect to the group chat management interface and automatically create relevant group chats based on ticket automation.,WPSCollaboration also provides the capability of bots, which can be used to automatically push messages to users, such as email notifications and department announcements.
