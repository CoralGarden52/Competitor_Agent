### [](https://blog.csdn.net/gitblog_00568/article/details/157194860)🛠️ 核心部署：三步完成私有化搭建

#### [](https://blog.csdn.net/gitblog_00568/article/details/157194860)第一步：创建Docker配置文件

在项目根目录新建`docker-compose.yml`文件：

```yaml
version: '3.8'
services:
  affine-selfhosted:
    image: ghcr.io/toeverything/affine:stable
    container_name: affine-private
    restart: unless-stopped
    ports:
      - "8080:3000"
    volumes:
      - affine_data:/app/storage
    environment:
      - AFFINE_SERVER_HOST=0.0.0.0
      - AFFINE_SERVER_PORT=3000
```
